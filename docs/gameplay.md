## Controls
### Basic Movement
- `W` - Move Up
- `S` - Move Down
- `A` - Move Left
- `D` - Move Right
- `SPACE` - Attack

### Combat
- `Left Click` - Attack
- `Right Click` - Magic Attack
- `Q` - Choice weapon
- `E` - Choice magic

### Interface
- `ESC` - Menu
